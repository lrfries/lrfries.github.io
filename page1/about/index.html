About Lucy Fries

Lucy Fries is a Magazine, News & Digital Journalism Major in the S.I Newhouse School of Public Communications at Syracuse University. She graduated from DePaul College Prep High School in Chicago Illinois in 2024 and is now a current sophmore. During her time in Highschool, she was a staff writer and sports section editor for her school's publication which sparked her love for writing. She decided to continue that path as a journalism major, and still integrates herself in on-campus publications. She is currently apart of University Girl, a student-run publication, working as a print and digital Graphic Designer. She is also apart of the editorial team for Fetch Collective, where she is a staff-writer covering culture, beauty, fashion, and news topics.
